How to Use X Quotes Generator

  1. Go to the Home page.
  2. Enter the URL of the tweet you want to screenshot.
  3. Click Generate Screenshot.
  4. Download or share the generated screenshot.

Requirements

  • A valid Twitter (X) URL.
  • An active internet connection.
